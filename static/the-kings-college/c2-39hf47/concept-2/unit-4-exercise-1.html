Genesis 1:27-31, 2:15-3:7 ESV

Apply the Principle Exercise

Use the diagram from the Make it Plain Exercise to match what is happening in the garden with Adam and Eve. Let's call it the "Roadmap to Death."

Discuss

Genesis 1:26-31, 2:15-3:7 ESV
Please do your best to answer the questions as simply as you can based on the text given and not any prior knowledge you may have, even if it's biblical.

Questions with asterisks* may inquire about general definitions and meaning not found in the text, for those you may use your common knowledge.
  • Were Adam and Eve created sinful?
  • According to Paul's Roadmap to death, when did sin come alive?
  • Are there any differences or steps left out in Adam and Eve's case?
  • What does the serpent represent here?
  • In verse 6, what was Eve's mind set on? What caused her mindset to shift?
  • Does there always need to be a "serpent" for this roadmap to sin to take place?
  • According to Paul, at what point can we stop this roadmap from happening?
  • Is there any other way to get out of the flesh sin cycle according to these scriptures?
  • A change occurred in verse 7, what was it according to Paul?
  • Once their eyes were opened, could they go back to the same state of their eyes being unopened?
